OUR STORY

Founded in 2023, SR.Restaurant emerged with a passion for delivering a unique blend of flavors, combining traditional BBQ, fast food, and the heartwarming tastes of Desi cuisine. Born out of a love for food and a commitment to quality, SR.Restaurant quickly became a local favorite. Our journey began with a small idea – to create a place where families and friends could enjoy great food in a relaxed atmosphere. Since then, we’ve grown from our first location into a beloved destination for food lovers of all kinds.
